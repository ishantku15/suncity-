About SunCity School

Established in 2010, SunCity School has been providing quality education in Linepar, Moradabad for over a decade. Affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), we nurture young minds from Nursery to Class 8 with a perfect blend of academic excellence and co-curricular activities.

Our mission is to create a stimulating learning environment that helps students discover their potential and develop into responsible global citizens. With a team of dedicated educators and state-of-the-art facilities, we ensure every child receives personalized attention and holistic development.

Our Academics

Comprehensive CBSE curriculum from Nursery to Class 8

Early Childhood Education

Our Nursery and Kindergarten program focuses on learning through play and exploration. We follow a child-centric approach that develops cognitive, linguistic, social, emotional and physical skills.

  • Activity-based learning
  • Phonics and early literacy
  • Numeracy skills development
  • Art, music and movement
  • Social and emotional development

Primary School Program

Classes 1 to 5 follow the CBSE curriculum with emphasis on building strong foundations in English, Hindi, Mathematics, Environmental Studies, and Computer Science.

  • Interactive smart class sessions
  • Language development programs
  • Mathematics lab activities
  • Science exploration
  • Computer literacy from Class 1

Middle School Program

Classes 6 to 8 prepare students for higher education with a structured CBSE curriculum that includes English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science, Social Science, and Computer Applications.

  • Subject specialist teachers
  • Practical lab experiences
  • Project-based learning
  • Career guidance sessions
  • Preparation for competitive exams
